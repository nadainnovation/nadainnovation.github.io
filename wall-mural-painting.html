Pricing

Pricing start at $20 per square feet with minimum of 50 sq ft. Discounts will apply to larger areas (> 250 sq ft). Price per square foot may increase if design requested is complex. This will be indicated in price quote and determined at the time that the final sketch is approved.

A non-refundable fee of $100 is due prior to sketch process and will be deducted from the total work fee. 25% non-refundable deposit is due 5 days prior to start date of painting (this allows ordering of the necessary supplies and for scheduling). Full balance is due on the final day of work completion.
